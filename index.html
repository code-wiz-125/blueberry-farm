Why Blueberries?

Blueberries are considered a superfood because of their nutritional value and antioxidant content.

They contain more antioxidants than any other common fruit or vegetable, and antioxidants have been linked to many health benefits, including:

Blueberries can help reduce high cholesterol,high blood pressure, artery stiffness, and inflammation.

Blueberries contain flavonoids and anthocyanins, which reduce inflammation and boost immunity.

Blueberries can help regulate mood, improve memory, and protect the brain from aging. Daily consumption has been found to slow memory impairments and motor coordination that are typically associated with aging.

Blueberries are packed with antioxidants, minerals, and fibers, and have been called a diabetic superfood by the American Diabetes Association.
