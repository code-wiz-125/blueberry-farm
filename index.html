Why Blueberries?

Blueberries are considered a superfood because of their nutritional value and antioxidant content.

They contain more antioxidants than any other common fruit or vegetable, and antioxidants have been linked to many health benefits, including:

Help reduce high cholesterol,high blood pressure, artery stiffness, and inflammation.

Contains flavonoids and anthocyanins, which reduce inflammation and boost immunity.

Help regulate mood, improve memory, and protect the brain from aging.

They're packed with antioxidants, minerals, and fibers, and have been called a diabetic superfood by the American Diabetes Association.
